About Me

This is what I do.

Here, we write about everything that tickles the human brain — from serious thoughts about life and technology to the kind of random musings that probably shouldn’t have made it past the group chat. Expect wit, wonder, and the occasional existential crisis wrapped in sarcasm and storytelling. We don’t promise wisdom, but we do promise entertainment. Whether you came here to learn something new, procrastinate productively, or just see how far words can be stretched before they break — you’re in the right place. So, grab a cup of filter coffee (or three), pull up a chair, and settle in. Because at Soona Paana, overthinking is a sport — and we play to win.

Every pixel, every route, every mysteriously broken CSS rule — all lovingly handcrafted through trial, error, and the occasional stack overflow meltdown. So if something on this site looks slightly “experimental,” that’s not a bug, it’s a feature. Soona Paana started as a coding project, but somewhere between debugging and deploying, it became a creative home — a place where tech experiments meet tangled thoughts. It’s proof that curiosity and caffeine can, in fact, build something worth visiting. So if you spot a typo, a weird layout, or a button that doesn’t do what you expected — just remember: that’s the charm of a work in progress... made by someone who’s still figuring it all out, one commit at a time.
