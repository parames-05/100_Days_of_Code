Meet The Creator

This platform was created by a human who believes keyboards are musical instruments and the spacebar deserves more appreciation. Fueled by caffeine, curiosity, and an unreasonable attachment to clean UI design, the creator decided that typing should feel less like homework and more like a boss battle. When not adjusting pixel shadows by one unit for “aesthetic reasons,” they can be found debating whether WPM is a sport, a philosophy, or a personality trait. This project is equal parts experiment, obsession, and love letter to the click-clack symphony of mechanical keys. If something looks slightly over-engineered, that was intentional. Probably.
